About us

Bikram's Liquor Shop is a based in Melbourne, locally owned & operated independent liquor store and we offer a wide range of products at competitive prices; our customers visit us for more than just our impressive selection of beer, wine and spirits. They come visit our family-owned store to experience friendly services and a sense of local community.

Bikram's Liqour Shop is your One Stop-Shop for all your liquor needs!

We are open 24/7 for orders and payment through our website! If your order received after trading hours we deliver in the next day!

We are helping you to choose the right products for the office party or picking the perfect gift for someone special our staff will gladly help you find what you need. Our aim to provide quick Liquor Home Delivery, click and collect and physically visit to our store with best customer satisfaction, excellent customer service and we never compromise the quality of the services provided to the customer.
